Empowering Communities Across Tanzania

Launched in 2023, Better Tomorrow is a transformative initiative aimed at empowering youth, women, and marginalized communities in Tanzania through digital literacy, entrepreneurship skills, and English proficiency. We are committed to building a strong and competitive generation prepared for the digital era.

Innovators from Better Tomorrow Cohort 1

Creators Group – Eco-Bamboo Project

Introducing bamboo charcoal as a sustainable alternative to traditional fuels, reducing deforestation and carbon emissions.

Team Members: Yusra Abdulbastwa, Aloyce Joseph, Elipidius Papius, Rugakingira

Innovation Hub – Intelligent Cargo Optimization System (ICOS)

Solving logistics inefficiencies by enabling vehicles to avoid returning empty through smart cargo matching.

Team Members: Madinah Samora, Victor Shirima, Baraka John, David Linga Mhagama

Jenga Link – Plastic Waste to Tiles

Transforming plastic waste into durable, eco-friendly construction tiles, promoting a circular economy and reducing pollution.

Team Members: Hussein I. Ukenge, Mashaka Marandu, Brian Mality, Hamfrey Jonas

Learning Hub – Nyumba Nyumbani

Helping house seekers find rental properties easily before and after arriving in the country using a digital housing system.

Team Members: Edna Joseph, Noah Tuzo, Teli Richard, Paul Jackson, Godbless, Recho Anord

Neon Nexius – EcoCycle Tanzania

Turning organic waste into fertilizer and sustainable animal feed, tackling pollution while supporting farmers and livestock keepers.

Team Members: Richard Garus, Nurdin Julius, Livinus Methodius, Shukran Richard

Karibu Better Tomorrow Jifunze Zaidi Kuhusu Huduma Zetu

Je, RootGIS inatoa programu gani?

RootGIS inatoa programu za kukuza ujuzi wa kidigitali, ujasiriamali, maarifa ya ramani (GIS), usimulizi wa hadithi za jamii, na mabadiliko ya tabianchi kupitia ubunifu na teknolojia.

Kwa nini Better Tomorrow iliundwa?

Better Tomorrow ni mpango wa kusaidia vijana, wanawake na makundi yaliyo pembezoni kwa kuwapatia maarifa ya kidigitali, Kiingereza, na ujasiriamali ili kuwajengea uwezo wa kushiriki kikamilifu kwenye maendeleo ya kidijitali nchini Tanzania.

Je, kuna gharama za kushiriki kwenye programu?

Programu nyingi zinatolewa bure au kwa mchango mdogo, hasa kwa makundi yaliyo katika mazingira magumu. Tunashirikiana na mashirika mbalimbali kusaidia kugharamia washiriki.

Je, programu hizi zinapatikana kwa kila mtu?

Ndiyo, zinapatikana kwa vijana, wanawake, na makundi mengine yanayohitaji msaada. Tunawakaribisha watu kutoka mikoa yote ya Tanzania kushiriki kwenye programu zetu kwa mizunguko ya kila miezi mitatu.

Je, mafunzo hutolewa kwa njia gani?

Mafunzo hutolewa ana kwa ana kupitia vituo kama American Corner, pamoja na warsha, bootcamps na mashindano ya ubunifu. Pia tunatumia njia za mtandaoni pale inapobidi.

Je, mna kutoa vyeti baada ya kumaliza programu?

Ndiyo. Washiriki wote wanaokamilisha programu hupatiwa vyeti kama uthibitisho wa ujuzi walioupata, ambao unaweza kuwasaidia kwenye ajira au uanzishaji wa biashara.
