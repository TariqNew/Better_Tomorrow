# **Better Tomorrow Program - Official Website**  

## **Overview**  
The **Better Tomorrow Program** is a transformative initiative dedicated to empowering individuals through education, mentorship, and skill-building. Our website serves as a hub for information about our programs, enrollment, and opportunities for engagement.

---

## **Features**  
✅ **Program Listings** – Explore various educational and skill-development programs.  
✅ **FAQs Section** – Get answers to common questions about program costs, eligibility, and more.  
✅ **Testimonials** – Hear from past participants about their experiences.  
✅ **Partners** – Learn about our key collaborators, including **American Space, Libe Green, RootGIS, and MTI**.  
✅ **Contact & Location** – Easily find us through integrated Google Maps and contact forms.

---

## **Technologies Used**  
- **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Bootstrap)  
- **Animations:** AOS (Animate on Scroll) & WOW.js  
- **Carousel:** Owl Carousel  
- **Icons:** Bootstrap Icons  
- **Map Integration:** Google Maps Embed API
